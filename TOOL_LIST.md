# 🏛️ The Architect's Tool List
**System Logic & Operational Infrastructure**
**Design by Irem Victor Chinonso**

This is the technical stack used to maintain the **Total Overlay** ecosystem. Every tool is selected for its ability to integrate into a mirrored data environment.

---

## 🧠 1. Core Intelligence & Data Science
*Tools used for designing statistical frameworks and validating data integrity.*

*   **Python:** The primary language for engineering custom normalization engines and data pipelines.
*   **R-Studio:** Utilized for advanced stochastic modeling and statistical probability analysis (OAU Framework).
*   **Pandas / NumPy:** Essential libraries for high-velocity data manipulation and index calculation.

## 🏛️ 2. Revenue Operations (RevOps) & CRM
*Systems used for lifecycle management and multi-market revenue tracking.*

*   **HubSpot (Certified):** The central source of truth for CRM, sales pipelines, and reporting architecture.
*   **Airtable:** High-relational database for tracking logistics and inventory with granular detail.
*   **Notion:** Knowledge architecture and "Intelligence Vaults" for OAU Statistics and Street Code assets.

## ⚡ 3. Automation & System Orchestration
*The "Connective Tissue" that ensures the Total Overlay (Sync) across all tools.*

*   **n8n:** Self-hosted automation for complex, multi-step business logic and data mirroring.
*   **Google Apps Script:** Custom JavaScript-based automation for real-time synchronization within Google Workspace.
*   **Zapia Max:** High-level AI orchestration for real-time decision support and task execution.

## 🎬 4. Creative Algorithmic Production
*Tools used for high-CPM content generation and "Street Code" cinematic distribution.*

*   **Runway ML (Gen-3 Alpha / Act-One):** Cutting-edge AI video generation for cinematic-grade visual assets.
*   **ElevenLabs:** High-fidelity voice synthesis for professional, low-key noir narration.
*   **InShot:** Mobile-optimized assembly line for final cinematic editing and distribution.
*   **Leonardo.ai / Kling AI:** Specialized engines for character consistency and hero-shot generation.

## 💹 5. Financial Strategy & Asset Tracking
*Tools for high-conviction updating and capital management.*

*   **Bybit:** Primary exchange for crypto asset management and statistical trading.
*   **Chipper Cash:** Virtual USD infrastructure for global credit and production funding.
*   **Trust Wallet:** Manual backup and security for long-term digital asset holding.
